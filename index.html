Every specimen below is a real pattern the bot blocks today: in message text, display names, image captions, and the text inside images.

Math-italic letters, fullwidth characters, and other Unicode tricks are normalized back to plain text before matching. The sender is banned and added to the global list.

Spaced letters

A D M I N

Display names with spaced-out letters are compacted and checked against 16 restricted role tags like admin, support, and dev.

Holder bait

Holders dm me

One of 100+ curated scam phrases and 12 pattern templates, tuned for crypto groups rather than generic profanity.

Lookalike admin

Аlex | Dev

That first letter is Cyrillic. Names are compared against your real admin list with confusables normalized and a fuzzy-match tolerance.

Contract drop

0x4f3a…e91b

ETH and Solana addresses are caught in messages, names, and images. Whitelist your own token's address; everything else escalates.

Wallet screenshot

Total Balance / Send / Receive / Swap

OCR reads every image and video keyframe. Wallet-app screenshots are scored by a weighted model, and known spam media is fingerprinted so a repost means an instant ban.

Four layers between
a scammer and your members

Screen at the door

With portal mode, every join request is pre-screened against the global banlist, restricted name tags, and impersonation checks before anyone gets in. Add an optional math or image captcha for the rest.

Read everything

Text, captions, quoted links, forwarded sources, image OCR, and hashed video keyframes. Renaming yourself mid-session does not help: identity checks run on every message, not just on join.

Escalate fairly

Honest members get a warning with a 10-second undo button, then mute, then ban. The ladder is configurable per group, and admins can clear all warnings on a user with one command.

Ban everywhere

High-confidence detections propagate to every guarded group, so a scammer caught once is gone everywhere. Only the bot's own detections feed the list, so a rogue admin cannot poison it.

The Watchtower reports every evening

You should not have to scroll back through the day to know what happened. Each evening at 20:00, every active group gets a short digest of the last 24 hours. Quiet groups are skipped instead of spammed with zeroes.

Full audit trail. Route bans, warnings, joins, and setting changes into a private log channel, filtered by category.

Filters

Custom auto-replies triggered by words or patterns, with media and buttons attached.

Timed messages

Recurring announcements on an interval you set, toggled on and off per post.

Quiet hours

Crowd reporting
